Section 2 — News and Catalyst Review
Macro — Oil / Geopolitical (Dominant Theme)
Brent crude $111.16/bbl (+2.71%), WTI approaching $100 (+3.98%) — 7th consecutive session of gains. US-Iran tensions have reduced Strait of Hormuz traffic to near zero, removing a route carrying ~20% of global energy flows. IEA has flagged an unprecedented supply shock risk scenario. Until diplomatic resolution or rerouting normalizes supply, oil remains the primary inflationary wildcard and multi-week structural theme.
FOMC Meeting (This Week)
Rates expected on hold. Key variable: does the Fed acknowledge oil-inflation pressure and signal rate-path caution? With Brent at $111, the inflation picture is more complex than 30 days ago. Statement language matters for premium-selling assumptions around yield-sensitive names.
AI Spending Narrative — OpenAI
WSJ report: OpenAI missed user growth and revenue targets; CFO Sarah Friar warned about compute contract funding. OpenAI denied. Market reaction reflects a structural concern — not company-specific — about whether AI revenue generation keeps pace with AI capital expenditure. Tonight's Mag-7 earnings put this question to the test.
