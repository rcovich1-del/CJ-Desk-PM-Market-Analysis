Closing Desk Note — Thursday, May 14

Thursday's session is defined by one event: the Trump-Xi summit. Everything else — VIX levels, sector rotation, the quality-versus-small-cap divergence — is context. SPY entering near its 52-week high reflects accumulated optimism around the trade truce and AI earnings cycle. The summit either validates or resets that optimism, and the answer comes in the pre-market. Approach the open with reduced gross exposure and no pre-loaded directional bets. Once the headline clears and the tone is established, execute the IC book in consumer staples (KMB, PG) and move to the HUM Bullish Diagonal if earnings timing confirms. Healthcare and consumer staples are the defensible premium zones in this tape. Technology and small caps carry the highest event-driven risk on both sides. The top focus for Thursday is building positions in areas where the macro supports the structure — and waiting for the event to tell you which version of Thursday you're trading.
